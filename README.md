Digital Task Management & Calendar System
Designed for FWMM Enterprises | Concept & Demo Project

Status: Planning & Demo Phase— This project was designed based on real operational challenges observed during my IT internship at FWMM Enterprises. Full development is planned.


The Problem

During my internship at FWMM Enterprises, I identified a major workflow issue:

- Supervisors wrote tasks on paper, took photos, and sent them via WhatsApp groups
- Task details got lost in chat history
- Employees received incomplete or unclear instructions
- There was no way to track progress, confirm deadlines, or see who was working on what
- Important updates were buried under dozens of unrelated messages

As the number of projects grew, this system became chaotic and unreliable.

---
The Solution

A web-based task management and calendar system that gives every employee a digital workspace — accessible from their phone, even on mobile data at remote construction sites.

---
Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
|  Employee Profiles | Each employee logs in with their personal profile, job role, and project history |
|  Task Calendar | New tasks appear instantly on the employee's calendar with full details |
|  Team Visibility | Employees can see who they are working with on each task |
|  Task Status Updates | Employees update progress, log challenges, and confirm completion |
|  Real-Time Tracking | Supervisors monitor all tasks in real time — no WhatsApp needed |
|  Mobile-First Design | Works on mobile data, designed for employees on construction sites |
